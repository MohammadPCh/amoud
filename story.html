About US
Amoud Design Studio

Amoud Design House is a luxury brand based in Iran, collaborating with AmiranStudio_art to create exquisite products and leading the field in collectible accessory design.
Amoud finds its roots in innovative and functional designs.
We transform high-quality materials into unforgettable collectible pieces for sophisticated environments, while also valuing their stories and experiences.

Our goal is to create a shared space, maintain sustainability and integrity, and produce exceptional products. We believe that furniture is both an art and an investment. Each product is custom-made, allowing us to minimize our environmental impact and connect with you on a personal level. When you design with us, you become part of the creation process. We involve you in every step, every decision, and every detail, making you feel as if you are right there in the workshop with us.

Furniture and accessories should be noticed and not left in a corner to be forgotten. Our products are designed with sustainable materials and unique aesthetics to inspire love for your spaces. Our accessories help tell your story and create new memories.

"Invest in memories."
The Amoud experience is a calm and holistic approach focused on feeling and connection. Together, we will create a sense of uniqueness that will become part of your journey.

Amoud was created by two architects, Hanieh and Ahmad. Both of us are educated and work in the field of architecture. After much discussion and contemplation, we decided to pursue our passion alongside our enjoyable architecture work and create a business that we could be as proud of as we are of our architectural achievements.
